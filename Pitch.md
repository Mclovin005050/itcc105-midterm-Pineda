# ASSCAT Campus AI Copilot

## Problem Statement
The current student portal is inefficient because it relies on a rigid, menu-based structure that forces users to manually search for services. 
Students often struggle to locate features like clearance, requests, or announcements due to poor navigation and unclear categorization. 
This results in wasted time, frustration, and increased dependency on staff for simple tasks.

## Industry 5.0 Justification
The ASSCAT Campus AI Copilot aligns with Industry 5.0 by prioritizing human-centered technology. Instead of replacing human roles, 
the AI acts as an intelligent assistant that enhances student and staff capabilities. It reduces repetitive inquiries, provides personalized guidance, 
and allows users to interact naturally through chat or voice. This empowers users to make faster decisions while keeping humans in control of critical processes.

## Agile Requirements (User Stories)
1. As a student, I want an AI assistant that understands my request, so that I can quickly access services without navigating menus.
2. As a faculty member, I want AI-generated summaries of student concerns, so that I can respond more efficiently.
3. As an administrator, I want the system to prioritize urgent requests automatically, so that critical issues are handled faster.
